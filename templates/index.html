About Bible Tabernacle

Bible Tabernacle PMB & DBN is a non-denominational Bible-believing church. We fellowship around the Word of God as revealed in this end-time season. We have no creed but Christ, no law but love, and no book but the Bible. We fellowship around the Word of God as revealed in this end-time season by the Holy Spirit.

We have been awakened by the great shepherds voice for our day made known through the ministry of Malachi 4:5-6; Revelations 10:7 and Luke 17:30. As Abraham sent Eliezer to get a bride, Rebecca for his only son Isaac, God sent a Prophet/Messenger, Reverend William Marrion Branham (1909-1965) to prepare a Bride for His only begotten Son, even our Lord Jesus Christ.

Reverend William Marrion Branham was one of the most influential Bible ministers of our time. He was considered by many to be the initiator of the healing and charismatic revival that began in 1947.

Most of the leaders of this revival considered William Branham its catalyst, and most of them drew their inspiration from his ministry to launch their own. But the ministry of William Branham, unparalleled in demonstration of power that Christ was working amongst His people, was not limited to praying for the sick and discerning the thoughts and intents of the heart. The revelation of Gods mystery truths and the Word becoming flesh in the lives of the believer was astounding.

From 1947 until the time of his passing in 1965, the powerful ministry of William Branham was well known and considered unparalleled in the history of gospel meetings. The impact of the supernatural ministry of this one man was felt not only in North America, but also around the world.
